# Achievement Skin Tones

Some GitHub achievements support skin tone customization based on your
Emoji Skin Tone Preference in GitHub settings.

## How to Change

1. Go to github.com/settings/appearance
2. Scroll to "Emoji skin tone preference"
3. Select your preferred skin tone
4. Achievement badges will update to match

## Affected Achievements

The following achievements feature human-like illustrations that
change based on your skin tone setting:

- Starstruck
- Quickdraw
- Pair Extraordinaire

## Available Skin Tones

GitHub follows the Unicode emoji skin tone standard:
- Default (yellow)
- Light
- Medium-Light
- Medium
- Medium-Dark
- Dark
